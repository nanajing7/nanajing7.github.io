About Me

Welcome! I'm a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Political Science at the Ohio State University. My research focuses on foreign lobbying, diplomacy, and international campaigns. My research uses various computational methods, including machine learning, large language models, neural networks, and social networks. You can find my package RAMEN on GitHub.
